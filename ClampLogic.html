ClampLogic

Specialized Pipeline Leak Detection & Clamp Design Engine

ClampLogic is a specialized engineering suite designed to bridge the gap between complex pipeline leak detection and rapid hardware manufacturing. By integrating ASME Section-VIII Div.1 and IS-18159 codes directly into its core logic, the application allows OEMs to generate manufacturing-ready designs for axial and radial leaks in minutes.

This ensures that every clamp produced - whether for toxic, corrosive, or cryogenic service - is backed by verified engineering intelligence.

Technology
Overview

For OEMs in the high-pressure piping industry, quality is synonymous with structural integrity. ClampLogic serves as a digital design partner that standardizes manufacturing outputs across all component types, including Straight Pipes, Elbows, Tees, and more.

By utilizing a proprietary Dynamic Engine, the application transforms user-defined fluid parameters and leak spatial data into precise detailed and rendered drawings and calculation summary reports.

Technical Architecture — Design Logic

ClampLogic v1.1 utilizes a multi-axial design engine to automate the transition from field data to shop-floor fabrication.

01

Defect-Specific Axial Optimization

Calculates sleeve length based on the specific axial extent of damage. Strictly adheres to the Minimum Length Rule, ensuring enclosure extends at least 5 x Pipe Dia.

02

Intelligent Enclosure Selection

Maps repair requirements against a comprehensive database of standard clamp lengths (6" to 24") and specialized high-diameter welded sleeves (5 to 10 feet).

03

Analytical Structural Integrity Guarantee

Performs rigorous analytical verification of hoop and axial stress capacities to ensure the design can withstand peak critical pressures.
