PipingLab

Advanced Piping Data Engineering Software

PipingLab enhances efficiency in piping design and removes burden of searching through fragmented engineering resources. It offers intuitive access to component catalogues, weight tables, parametric shapes, and design utilities, helping engineers and researchers accelerate workflows while ensuring accuracy. Data can be seamlessly configured in both Metric and U.S. Customary Units for global applicability.

Its piping databases and parametric models are widely used in 3D plant design environments, engineering simulations, and software platforms, making it an essential tool for professionals worldwide.

PipingLab Capabilities

Engineered for precision, built for scale.

01

📚 Comprehensive Data Libraries

This system features extensive databases for pipes, fittings, flanges, valves, and specialty items, fully compliant with ASME, ASTM, API, and MSS codes. It integrates critical material properties, including pressure-temperature ratings, allowable stresses, and standardized weight tables with built-in calculation utilities. Designed for global projects, it provides seamless support for both Metric (SI) and U.S. Customary units.

02

📐 Parametric Component Modeling

This system features intelligent, rule-based component generation with automated dimensioning and real-time validation against industry standards. It allows for highly configurable parameters to meet specific custom project requirements while ensuring strict compliance and precision throughout the design process.

03

⚙️ Piping Material Specification (PMS)

Efficiently create and manage project-specific PMS libraries by defining piping classes, materials, sizes, schedules, and pressure ratings tailored to specific service conditions. By applying industry design codes and standards, this system ensures absolute consistency across design, procurement, and construction phases. This streamlined approach significantly reduces engineering errors, rework, and approval cycles, ensuring a synchronized project workflow.

04

🏭 Industry Applications & EPC Support

Optimized for Oil & Gas, Chemical, and Petrochemical facilities, this system provides comprehensive support for Process and Utility Piping Systems. It is specifically designed to streamline EPC (Engineering, Procurement, & Construction) projects and empower Engineering Research & Development teams by ensuring technical precision across all project lifecycle stages.

05

🌐 Digital Twin & Workflow Integration

This system offers seamless integration with 3D plant design environments and modern engineering workflows, providing structured data outputs for downstream tools and digital twins. It ensures full interoperability with major engineering platforms and remains consistently reliable through regular updates aligned with the latest international codes and standards.

06

🚀 Compliance & Future-Ready Updates

This system ensures continuous accuracy through regular updates aligned with the latest international codes and standards (ASME, API, etc.). By maintaining a synchronized database with global regulatory changes, it guarantees technical compliance and future-readiness for your projects, effectively mitigating risks associated with outdated specifications and ensuring long-term operational reliability.
