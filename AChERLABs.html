PipingLab

The Definitive Digital Standards & Specification Engine

PipingLab enhances efficiency in piping design and removes burden of searching through fragmented engineering resources. It offers intuitive access to component catalogues, weight tables, parametric shapes, and design utilities, helping engineers and researchers accelerate workflows while ensuring accuracy. Data can be seamlessly configured in both Metric and U.S. Customary Units for global applicability.

Developed by Acherlabs, PipingLab has quickly established itself as a trusted resource in oil & gas, chemical, and process industries. Its piping databases and parametric models are widely used in 3D plant design environments, engineering simulations, and software platforms, making it an essential tool for professionals worldwide.

PipingLab Capabilities

Engineered for precision, built for scale.

01

Comprehensive Data Intelligence

The PipingLab ecosystem integrates an exhaustive digital repository of pipes, fittings, flanges, valves, and specialty components. Every asset is strictly indexed for full compliance with ASME, ASTM, API, and MSS codes. Beyond geometry, the system provides integrated material properties—including pressure-temperature ratings and allowable stresses—paired with standardized weight tables and native calculation utilities. Engineered for global deployment, it offers seamless, high-precision support for both Metric (SI) and U.S. Customary units.

02

Parametric Design & Validation

Experience intelligent, rule-based component generation driven by automated dimensioning and real-time validation against rigorous industry standards. PipingLab enables highly configurable parametric modeling to meet bespoke project requirements while ensuring absolute compliance throughout the design phase. By bridging the gap between custom engineering and standardized precision, the system eliminates manual errors and accelerates the path from conceptual design to operational procurement.

03

Piping Material Specification (PMS)

PipingLab empowers engineers to architect project-specific Piping Material Specification (PMS) libraries with surgical precision. By defining rigorous piping classes, material grades, schedules, and pressure ratings tailored to specific service conditions, the system enforces absolute technical consistency across the design, procurement, and construction phases. This centralized approach drastically mitigates engineering discrepancies, reduces rework, and accelerates approval cycles for a synchronized project workflow.

04

Valve Material Specifications (VMS)

The VMS module provides a specialized framework for the high-fidelity definition of valve assemblies. It automates the generation of comprehensive Valve Material Specifications, incorporating granular data on body metallurgy, trim configurations, pressure-temperature ratings, and end connections. By aligning every valve component with global design codes, PipingLab ensures that procurement and engineering teams remain perfectly calibrated on critical asset requirements.

05

Digital Twin & Ecosystem Interoperability

PipingLab offers seamless integration with high-fidelity 3D plant design environments and modern computational workflows. By providing structured, high-order data outputs, it feeds directly into Digital Twins and downstream engineering tools. The system ensures full interoperability with major industrial platforms, maintaining a single source of truth that remains reliable through continuous synchronization with the latest global engineering standards.

06

Future-Ready Regulatory Compliance

Stay ahead of the regulatory curve with a system that ensures continuous alignment with evolving international codes and standards (ASME, API, ISO, and more). By maintaining a live, synchronized database that reflects global regulatory shifts, PipingLab guarantees technical compliance and future-readiness for your assets. This proactive methodology effectively mitigates the risks associated with specification drift, ensuring long-term operational reliability and safety.
